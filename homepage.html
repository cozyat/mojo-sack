Each year, 119 billion pounds of food is wasted in the United States.

At MojoSack, we are aware of the tons of food waste that is produced by eateries and restaurants around the world.

To mitigate this crisis, we came up with the idea of MojoSack where we save fresh food like desserts that would otherwise be thrown out.

Not only do customers save their money, but they also save our planet from climate change and from the buildup of food waste!
